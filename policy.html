Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

1. Introduction

Welcome to CloudCover Insurance. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website and submit your details for an insurance quotation.

2. Information We Collect

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • National ID/Passport Number
  • Vehicle Registration Details (if applicable)
  • Insurance Preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide you with accurate insurance quotations
  • To communicate with you regarding your inquiry
  • To process and facilitate insurance applications
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To improve our services and customer experience

4. Data Protection and Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our website uses encryption and other security protocols to ensure the confidentiality of your data.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for us to process your data
  • Request details on how your data is being used
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority
