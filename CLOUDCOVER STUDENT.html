What is Offered?

INTERNCARE provides compensation to interns and students on industrial attachment in case of injury or disability caused by violent, accidental, external, and visible events.

Apply Now

What it covers

Medical Expenses Our comprehensive coverage ensures that you're protected in case of accidents during your internship or attachment. We cover all necessary medical treatments, from emergency care to follow-up visits, giving you peace of mind to focus on your professional development.
Accidental Dental Expenses Dental emergencies can happen unexpectedly. We provide specialized coverage for dental treatments required due to accidents, ensuring your smile stays protected. Our response time is guaranteed within 48 hours of the incident.
Permanent Disability In the unfortunate event of permanent disability caused by workplace accidents, we provide comprehensive financial support to help you adapt and maintain your quality of life. Our coverage includes both partial and total disability scenarios.
Accidental Death We understand the importance of family security. Our accidental death coverage provides financial protection for your loved ones, ensuring they receive adequate support during difficult times. The claim process is streamlined for quick disbursement.
Last Expense Our last expense coverage helps families manage funeral arrangements without financial strain. We provide immediate assistance and support to ensure dignified arrangements can be made with minimal stress on the family.
