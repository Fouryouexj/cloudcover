What is Offered?

INTERNCARE provides compensation to interns and students on industrial attachment in case of injury or disability caused by violent, accidental, external, and visible events.

What it covers

Medical Expenses:Comprehensive coverage for medical treatment resulting from accidents.

Accidental Dental Expenses:Assistance for dental treatments due to accidents. 48 hours after an accident resulting in death. This ensures timely support during difficult times.

Permanent Disability: Financial support in case of permanent disability caused by accidents.

Accidental Death: Providing financial relief to the family in the event of the insured's accidental death.

Last Expense: Assisting with the expenses related to the insured's funeral arrangements.
