What is Micro Medical Insurance?

Micro medical insurance is a specialized form of health coverage designed to provide affordable healthcare solutions to low-income individuals and families. It is tailored to meet the needs of those who may not have access to conventional health insurance due to financial constraints, lack of awareness, or geographical limitations.

Key Features of Micro Medical Insurance:

  • Affordability: Low premiums for accessibility.
  • Simplified Processes: Minimal documentation & easy claims.
  • Community-Based Models: Works through cooperatives & microfinance groups.
  • Limited Coverage: Covers outpatient care, hospitalization, minor surgeries.
  • Flexible Payment Plans: Small installment payments to fit irregular incomes.
